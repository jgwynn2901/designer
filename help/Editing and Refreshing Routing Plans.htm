Editing and Refreshing Routing Plans

You have several choices at the routing plan level on the Business Entity Summary page. You can edit or refresh a routing plan.

Editing Routing Plans enables you to change or update a routing plans. If your routing plan needs revising, you may want to use Editing Routing Plans.

Refreshing Call Flows enables you to update routing plans with the latest information. If your routing plan needs the most recent information from the server, you may want to use Refreshing Routing Plans.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Editing and Refreshing Routing Plans.

Navigation Details

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierarchy tree.

3. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to go to the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

4. Click the "+" sign next to Routing Plans on the Business Entity page.
