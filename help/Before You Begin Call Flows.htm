Before You Begin Call Flows

"Before You Begin Call Flows" contains conceptual information you should know before starting Call Flows. These topics provide brief overviews of important concepts used in building call flows.

"Designer and Call Flows" gives a brief summary of the Designer and how it is used to create call flows. "About Call Flows" tells about call flows in general. "Standard Call Flows" describes the advantages of using standard call flows and "Sharing Call Flows" describes the advantages of sharing call flows.

At the call flow component level you also have two choices. "Sharing Call Flow Components" explains the consequences of sharing call flow components. "Creating Call Flow Components" describes the advantage of creating call flow components.

Call Flow Common Tasks is a list of links to common tasks you may do when building a call flow.
