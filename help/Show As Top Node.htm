Show As Top Node

Show As Top Node moves the client, risk location or account to the top of the Hierarchy Tree.

Click on Navigation Details for information on how to get to Show As Top Node.

Navigation Details

1. On the Designer navigation bar, click Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS on the Hierarchy Tree.

3. Select a client, risk location or account on the Hierarchy Tree.

  • Right click the mouse on the client (The party that contracts FNS for the service), risk location (The local physical address(es) covered by the insured's policy) or account (Any level in the Account Hierarchy that is specified to be of that type).

  • Click "Show As Top Node" on the popup menu.

Note

Show As Top Node is a temporary change.
