Attach Call Flow

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierarchy Tree.

3. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to display the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

4. Click the "+" sign next to Account Call Flows on the Business Entity page.

5. Select call flow from call flows list.

6. Click the "Attach" button on the Account Call Flow bar.
