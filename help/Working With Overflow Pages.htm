Working With Overflow Pages

The overflow page is used to capture information taken during the call that does not fit on the First Report Of Injury.

In routing plans the overflow page is just a placeholder. What information maps to the overflow page is determined by the output overflow table in the maintenance menu. You attach an overflow page the same way you do an output page.

Example of a placeholder field on an overflow page

 

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Working with Overflow Pages.

Navigation Details

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierarchy tree.

3. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to go to the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

4. Click the "+" sign next to the Routing Plans on the Business Entity page.

5. Select routing plan from list of routing plans and click "Edit" on the Routing Plans bar.

6. Double click Transmission Sequence Step and double click Output Item.

7. Right click the mouse on the Output Definition and select "Visual Editor" from the popup menu.

Adding Overflow Pages

Adding Overflow Pages

  • Filter by line of business and client.

  • Select the New button to add an attribute.

  • lookup the attribute to be added.

  • Select attribute.

  • Add sequence and caption.

Caption length equals 40. Show when empty. Yes for headers and no for data.

  • Attach client and line of business.

  • Click the Save button.
