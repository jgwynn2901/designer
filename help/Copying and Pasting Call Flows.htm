Copying and Pasting Call Flows

You have several choices at the call flow level on the Business Entity Summary page. You can copy call flows and paste call flows.

Whether you want to copy call flows and paste call flows depends on how you want to use the call flow. If you plan on making changes to the call flow, you may want to use a copied call flow (A copied call flow is a duplicate of a call flow in a database. It is not shared by other call flows.).

Copying call flows allows you greater flexibility because you are using duplicates of call flows. This allows you to modify the copy of the call flow to suit your needs.

Therefore you should copy a call flow if you know of another call flow that has most of the same informational requirements. For example, if you need a Worker's Compensation call flow for AIG you might want to copy the Gallagher Bassett Worker's Compensation call flow and change it to exactly meet your informational needs.

Once the call flow is copied you'll want to paste call flow (A pasted call flow is a call flow moved from the Windows Clipboard to its new location). After you have pasted the call flow you edit the call.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Copying and Pasting Call Flows.

Navigation Details

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierarchy tree.

3. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to go to the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

4. Click the "+" sign next to the Account Call Flows on the Business Entity page.

Copying Call Flows

Copying Call Flows

Select call flow from call flows list.

Click "Copy" on the Account Call Flows bar.

Pasting Call Flows

Pasting Call Flows

Select the client you want to paste the call flow to in the Hierarchy Tree and click the "+" sign next to Account Call Flows on the Business Entity Summary page.

Click the "Paste" button on the Account Call Flows bar.

Click "OK" on the "Are you sure you want to copy call flow?" dialog box.

Note

You must copy the call flow to the clipboard before you can paste the call flow from the clipboard into the Business Entity Summary page.
