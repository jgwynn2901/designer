Copying and Pasting Routing Plans

You have several choices at the routing plan level on the Business Entity Summary page. You can copy routing plans and paste routing plans.

Copying Routing Plans enables you to copy a routing plan. Once the routing plan is copied, you can paste the routing plan into another routing plan.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Copying and Pasting Routing Plans.

Navigation Details

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierachy Tree.

3. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to go to the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

4. Click the "+" sign next to Routing Plans on the Business Entity page.

Common Tasks

These Common Tasks are used when Copying and Pasting Routing Plans. Click the green text for detailed information on each task.
