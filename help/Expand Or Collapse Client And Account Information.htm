Expand Or Collapse Client And Account Information

Expand or collapse a branch to see only the information you want to see.

Click on Navigation Details for information on how to get to Expand Or Collapse Client And Account Information.

Navigation Details

1. On the Designer navigation bar, click Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS on the Hierarchy Tree.

3. Select a client, risk location or account on the Hierarchy Tree.

Click the "+" sign next to the client (The party that contracts FNS for the service.), risk location (The local physical address(es) covered by the insured's policy) or account (any level in the Account Hierarchy that is specified to be of that type) to expand the branch. Click the "-" sign next to the client, risk location or account to collapse the branch.
