About FNSNet Designer

FNSNet Designer is a tool used by business analysts to design business entities, data and processing rules for FNSNet.

The FNSNet Designer enables you to:

  • Design and maintain business entities in Account Hierarchy

  • Assign, Edit and Create Call Flows, Routing Plans, Ouput Definitons, Policies and Processing Rules for business entities in Account Hierarchy

  • Design and edit the layout of Call Flow screens and Fax/ Print Ouptut Pages

  • Maintain Branch Assignments, Risk Locations, Routing Destinations and User Profiles
