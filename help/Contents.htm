Contents

The Contents tab lists the "books" or "topics" in Designer Help. It is used for browsing Designer Help. If you don't have a rough idea of the topic you are looking for and where it might be referenced in Designer Help use Contents.

To view topics within a book, double-click the book so that its icon changes to an open book and its contents (additional books and topics) are displayed. A question mark icon indicates specific topics. Double-click any topic to view it. To close a book, if needed, also double-click it.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Contents.

Navigation Details

1. From the Designer Navigation bar, choose the Help button.

2. In the left frame click the Contents tab.

In the left frame double click the Designer Help book to expand it and double click the book you want to read. Click the topic you want to read.

The topic you clicked appears in the right frame.

Note

You can also expand a branch in the left frame by clicking the plus sign. You can collapse a branch by clicking the minus sign.

Use the scroll bars at the bottom of the left frame to scroll left and right. Use the scroll bars on the right side of the right frame to scroll up and down a topic.
