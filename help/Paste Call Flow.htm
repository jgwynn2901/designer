Paste Call Flow

1. Select the client you want to paste the call flow to in the Hierarchy Tree.

2. Click the "+" sign next to Account Call Flows on the Business Entity Summary page

3. Click the "Paste" button on the Account Call Flows bar.

4. Click "OK" on the "Are you sure you want to copy call flow?" dialog box.

Note

You must copy the call flow to the clipboard before you can paste the call flow from the clipboard into the Business Entity Summary page. See "Copy Call Flow" in the help for details.
