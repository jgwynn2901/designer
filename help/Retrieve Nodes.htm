Retrieve Nodes

Retrieve Nodes gets nodes that were not displayed under a parent node due to the number of nodes.

Click on Navigation Details for information on how to get to Retrieve Nodes.

Navigation Details

1. On the Designer navigation bar, click Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS on the Hierarchy Tree.

3. Select a client, risk location or account on the Hierarchy Tree.

  • Double click on any "..." node to retrieve the nodes.
