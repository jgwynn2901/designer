About Call Flows and Claim Entry

About Call Flows and Claim Entry

Claim Entry is used to collect claim information by the call representative from the caller. It is part of FNSNet.

The call representative uses call flows to obtain claim information from the caller. The call flow (A call flow is a group of screens that collect or lookup data as the call representative is taking the call.) requests claim information required to complete a particular type of call. It is specific to a line of business and is unique for each client. For example the "Workers Compensation" call flow requests information required to correctly file a "Worker's Compensation Claim." The "Commercial Liability" call flow requests information required to correctly file a "Commercial Liability" claim.

Call Flow Components In Designer

Call Flow Components In Designer

You use the Designer to create the call flow the call representative uses in Claim Entry. A call flow consists of these components:

  • Frames (A frame is a screen or page requesting information such as "Caller Information" or "Loss Location.")

  • Attributes (An attribute is a field such as "First Name" or "Zip Code.")

  • Rules (A rule is a formula that makes something happen to frames and attributes.)
