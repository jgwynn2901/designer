Index

The Index tab lists the words indexed in this help. If you have a rough idea of the topic you're looking for and how it might be referenced in Designer Help use the Index.

You type all or part of the topic you want information about in the text box. The list below the text box scrolls to display the topics that most closely match your entry. Whenever you see an entry you want to view in the list double-click the entry to see it. You can also highlight the entry and click the "Display" button to see it.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Index.

Navigation Details

1. From the Designer Navigation bar, choose the Help button.

2. In the left frame click the Index tab.

Type the word or words you are looking for in the index and press the enter key.

The topic for the word is displayed in the right frame.

Note

You can also use the scroll bar at the right side of the left frame to scroll through all the words in the index. Double click on the word you want and the topic appears in the right frame.
