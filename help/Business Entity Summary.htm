Business Entity Summary

The Business Entity Summary page displays a summary of a business entity's items. These items are:

  • Account Call Flows

  • Routing Plans

  • Policy

  • Billing

  • Branch Assignment Types

You use the Business Entity Summary page to do the following to the items listed above.

  • New

  • Edit

  • Copy

  • Paste

  • Filter

  • Delete

  • Attach

  • Refresh

For example, if you wanted to create a new Account Call Flow you would start on the Business Entity Summary page. Or if you wanted to edit an Account Call Flow you would start on the Business Entity Summary page.

You can also access Alternate Names and Account Details from the Business Entity Summary page. Alternate Name enables you to create an alternate name for a business entity. Account Details enables you to view or change account information.

You can expand all information on the page by clicking the green "+" icon in the upper right corner or collapse all information on the page by clicking the green "-" sign in the upper right corner.
