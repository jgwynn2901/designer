Add To Favorites

Add To Favorites copies a client, risk location or account to "Favorites" located underneath the Hierarchy Tree. This gives you fast access to a particular client, risk location or account without having to find the information on the Hierarchy Tree.

Add To Favorites is used to make a collection of your favorite clients, risk locations or accounts that you use frequently.

Click on Navigation Details for information on how to get to Add To Favorites.

Navigation Details

1. On the Designer navigation bar, click Hierarchy.

2. Click the "+" sign next to FNS on the Hierarchy Tree.

3. Select a client, risk location or account on the Hierarchy Tree.

  • Right click the mouse on the client (The party that contracts FNS for the service.), risk location (The local physical address(es) covered by the insured's policy) or account (Any level in the Account Hierarchy that is specified to be of that type.).

  • Click "Add To Favorites" on the popup menu.

Notes

Temporary Add To Favorites or Permanent Add To Favorites

Whether the Add To Favorites is temporary or permanent depends on a setting in the Settings page dialog box. If you want a temporary Add To Favorites:

  • On the Designer Navigation bar, choose Settings.

  • Make sure the "Add Favorites" check box is unmarked.

  • Click "Save Settings" button on the Settings Page dialog box.

  If you want a permanent Add To Favorites:

  • On the Designer Navigation bar, choose Settings.

  • Check "Add Favorites" on the Settings page dialog box.

  • Click "Save Settings" button on the Settings Page dialog box.
