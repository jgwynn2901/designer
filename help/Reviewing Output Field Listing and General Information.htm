Reviewing Output Field Listing and General Information

You also have the option of reviewing Output Field Listing and General Information in the Output Definition Editor.

Output Field Listing is a list of output fields on a page. It displays Name, X Position, Y Position, Width and Height. General Information enables editing of general information about a page such as Name and Background bitmap.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Reviewing Output Field Listing and General Information.

Navigation Details

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy and click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierarchy Tree.

2. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to display the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

3. Click the "+" sign next to Routing Plans on the Business Entity Summary page.

4. Select routing plan from list of routing plans and click the Edit button on the Routing Plan bar.

5. Right click the mouse on the Output Definition and select Visual Editor from the popup menu.
