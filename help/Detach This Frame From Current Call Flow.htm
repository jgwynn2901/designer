Detach This Frame From Current Call Flow

1. Click the Detach This Frame From Current Call Flow icon near the Frame Name in the upper left corner of the Call Flow Editor.

2. Click OK on the "Are you sure you want to detach frame?" dialog box.

Note

The detached frame disappears in the Call Flow Frames drop down list located at the top of the Call Flow Editor. You can also minimize the Call Flow Editor and notice the detached frame is gone from the Call Flow Frames area.
