Reviewing Attribute Instance Listing and General Information

You also have the option of reviewing Attribute Instance Listing and General Information.

Attribute Instance Listing is a list of attribute instances on a frame. It displays Instance Name, Sequence, Type, Mandatory, Width and Height. General Information enables editing of general information about a frame such as Name, Title, Help String, Description and Attribute Prefix.

Click Navigation Details for information on how to get to Reviewing Attribute Instance Listing and General Information.

Navigation Details

1. From the Navigation bar, choose Hierarchy and click the "+" sign next to FNS in the Hierarchy Tree.

2. Select the client in the Hierarchy Tree to display the Business Entity Summary page in the right frame.

3. Click the "+" sign next to Account Call Flows on the Business Entity Summary page.

4. Select call flow from list of call flows and click the Edit button on the Account Call Flows bar.

5. Right click the mouse on the call flow in the Call Flow Frames section and select Visual Editor from the popup menu.
