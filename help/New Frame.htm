New Frame

1. Click the New Frame icon in the upper left corner of the Call Fow Editor.

2. Enter or select the New Call Flow Frame information below.

  • Enter Name and Title.

  • Enter Help String.

The Help String is the information in the Tips frame in the call flow screen. The Tips frame contains suggestions for taking the information from the caller. For example, the Tips frame in the Caller Information screen could read "(If the caller is someone who has called previuosly, perform a caller lookup to access our database of clients.)"

  • Enter Description.

  • Enter Attribute Prefix, Type and Maximun Results Rows.

3. Click the Save button.
